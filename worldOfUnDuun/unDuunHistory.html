First came the Orcs, Made to tame the world.
Plants and trees bloomed across the land and so too were born the Fae and their Kin the Elven people.
Mountains rose to the sky, valleys were split and born were the Dragonkin.
The seas rose and washed rivers and lakes across the land with these waters came the Dainfolk.
The fire of the world itself spit from the mountains and so were born the Plane-touched.
The many people thrived and magic rose into the world and so came the half-folk.
All was made and all was good until a darkness bled into the world.
Anger and jealousy grew until the world divided into nations.
Darkness took hold and the sky was lit afire, the lands were split, and empires fell.
The world has rebuilt but the darkness remains and is rising again.
